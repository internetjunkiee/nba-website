Gordon Hayward was born on March 23, 1990 in Indianapolis, Indiana, USA as Gordon Daniel Hayward. He Scored seven points in 40 seconds in the first quarter of game 6 of the 2017 NBA playoffs. He is a small forward with the Utah Jazz (2010-2017) and Boston Celtics (2017-present). He also fractured his left ankle after an attempt for an alley-oop in his first game as a member of the Boston Celtic.[Oct 2017].
